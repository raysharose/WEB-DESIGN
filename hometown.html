About Kochi

Kochi, also known as Cochin, is a beautiful coastal city in Kerala. It is famous for its backwaters, beaches, and rich cultural heritage.

Famous Places

The Chinese Fishing Nets at Fort Kochi are one of the most iconic sights in the city.

Delicious Food

Kerala cuisine is known for its spices and flavors. Don't miss the traditional Sadya served on banana leaves!
